You and your friends get lost trying to take a shortcut through the forest and begin to worry because it's getting dark. You find a trail and decide to see where it leads, hoping for a way home. When you reach the end you can see a gigantic, creepy looking mansion sitting up on the hill. The roof is caving in slightly, many of the windows are broken and the lawn is overgrown like no one has been there for years. Your friend Paul suggests that you should go in and check it out and everyone agrees. You don't want them to know you're scared and make fun of you so you walk up to the front door. It slowly creaks open and you walk in. As soon as you are inside, the door slams shut behind you and you hear it lock. You keep trying to open it, but the door won't budge......
